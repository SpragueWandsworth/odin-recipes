Tomato Soup

Description

This delicious simple to make tomato soup is perfect for a cold day when you're

running low on time and need to whip up something simple yet tasty.

Ingredients

  • 150ml boiling water
  • 2 tins of chopped tomatoes
  • 50ml fresh cream
  • 6 large tomatoes
  • 10 ml milk
  • garnish of choice

Instructions

  1. Pour your chopped tomatoes into a large saucepan along with your water and cook until it starts to bubble.
  2. Once bubbling dice all of the tomatoes and add them to the mix to be cooked until soft.
  3. Then add both the milk and cream then stir it into the soup.
  4. Leave to cook for about 15 minutes or until the diced tomatoes are nearly disolved, making sure to stir regularly.
  5. Lastly pour the soup into a bowl, then garnish and enjoy.
