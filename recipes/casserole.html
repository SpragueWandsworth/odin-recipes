Tuna Casserole

Description

A comforting, creamy and easy to make casserole packed full of delicious tuna to

keep you warm on a cold day. Can be served with a salad of choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of mushroom soup
  • 4 cans of tuna
  • 1/2 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 a chopped onion
  • 100g of frozen peas
  • 400g of pasta of choice

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees C.
  2. Pour both cans of mushroom into a large saucepan and heat until it starts to bubble.
  3. Add in your 1/2 can of chopped tomatoes and stir until they combine with the mushroom soup.
  4. Add all 4 cans of tuna, your 1/2 chopped onion and frozen peas, stir, then leave to cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Once the 5 minutes is up add your pasta of choice, stir, then leave to soften for 15 minutes or until soft.
  6. Now that the pasta is cooked pour all of the mixture into a baking dish then put it in the preheated oven for 45 minutes.
  7. When the 45 minutes is up you may take your baking dish out of the oven and serve the casserole on a plate with a salad of choice.
  8. Enjoy!
