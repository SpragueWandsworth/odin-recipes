Tuna Casserole

Description

A comforting, creamy and easy to make casserole packed full of delicious tuna to keep you warm on a cold day. Can be served with a salad of choice.

Ingredients

    2 cans of mushroom soup 4 cans of tuna 1/2 can of chopped tomatos 1/2 a chopped onion 1 large egg 100g of frozen peas
