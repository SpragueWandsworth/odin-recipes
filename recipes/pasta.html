Chicken Pasta

Description

This Chicken Pasta is super simple and cheap to make yet so good it'll always

leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta of choice
  • 200g chicken breast
  • 25g melted butter
  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 4 tablespoons pesto of choice
  • 50g butter beans
  • 25g chopped garlic
  • herbs of choice

Instructions

  1. Start by cooking your chicken in a large frying pan until brown around the edges.
  2. Next put your pasta on and leave it until it has boiled.
  3. Drain and transfer the boiled pasta into the frying pan that contains the chicken.
  4. Add your butter and pesto of choice then mix it into the chicken and pasta.
  5. Add your chopped tomatoes, butter beans and garlic then stir and leave until the chopped tomatoes begin to bubble.
  6. Once it's bubbling leave to cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Turn the heat of and serve in a bowl, finish by sprinkling the herbs over the top and enjoy.
